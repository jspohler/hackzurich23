FrostDB
FrostDB is an embedded columnar DBMS written in Go designed for observability time-series data sets.
https://www.polarsignals.com/blog/posts/2022/05/04/introducing-arcticdb/
History
The original ArcticDB system was renamed to FrostDB in 2022 due to a trademark conflict.
https://www.polarsignals.com/blog/posts/2022/06/16/arcticdb-is-now-frostdb/
